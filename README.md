## 📊 Project Description
This Power BI dashboard is designed to perform predictive analytics using historical sales and advertising data.  
The dashboard provides interactive visualizations to analyze sales performance, profit trends, advertising impact, and ROI for business decision-making.

---

# 🔍 Dashboard Analysis

## 1. Sales Trend by Year
The line chart shows yearly sales performance from 2020 to 2022.

### Insights:
- Sales remained stable around **199K** in 2020 and 2021.
- A significant decline occurred in 2022 where sales dropped to **147K**.

---

## 2. Sales vs Advertising Spend
The scatter plot represents the relationship between advertising spend and sales.

### Insights:
- Higher advertising spend generally leads to higher sales.
- Some campaigns show higher spend with lower sales, indicating optimization opportunities.

---

## 3. Sales Category Distribution
The bar chart displays sales categories: Low, Medium, and High.

### Insights:
- Most records belong to the **Low Sales Category**.
- High sales frequency is comparatively lower.

---

## 4. Monthly Sales Analysis
The horizontal bar chart shows monthly sales performance.

### Insights:
- Highest monthly sales reached around **56K**.
- Lowest monthly sales were around **32K–35K**.
